Audio Setup

This task requires precision timing to match a sound with your heartbeat.

⚠️ Do not use Bluetooth headphones.

Wireless headphones introduce an audio delay that invalidates the data.

Please use wired earphones or the built-in device speaker in a quiet room.

Sensor Preparation

Place the tip of your index finger gently over both the rear camera lens and the flashlight.

Keep your finger perfectly still. We are measuring your pulse optically, so movement will disrupt the reading.

Calibration Complete

In the main task, you will hear a series of tones. Each tone represents one of your heartbeats, but the timing may be shifted.

Rotate the dial until the tone feels perfectly in sync with your actual heartbeat.

Task Instructions

Do not actively try to feel your pulse with your other hand. We want to measure your internal perception.

Keep your finger steadily on the camera throughout the task.

You will start with 2 practice trials.
